主题：不要在送餐时使用塑料

社会意义：塑料制品需要10到1000年的时间分解成微塑料，然后通过地下水流入大海。它们通过海洋食物链回到人类身上。因此减少塑料垃圾具有紧迫性。
Plastic takes 10 to 1,000 years to decompose into microplastics, which flow through groundwater into the sea. They return via the marine food chain to people. So reducing plastic waste is urgent.

背景：环境保护署将于2020年年底完成一次性塑料餐具管制研究，并提出建议。世界自然基金会敦促政府在2022年之前逐步淘汰所有一次性塑料容器。
The Environmental Protection Department will complete a study on the control of disposable plastic cutlery in late 2020, with recommendations. The WWF urged the government to set a phaseout of all single-use plastic cultery by 2022.

信息2：2018年，香港弃置的塑料餐具约占都市固体废物(MSW)总量的31%，这些塑料餐具将被送往垃圾填埋场或海洋。根据世界自然基金会香港海岸观察报告，餐具、容器和吸管等一次性餐具是香港十大海洋垃圾来源之一。
In 2018, Hong Kong disposed of about 31 per cent of its municipal solid waste (MSW) - plastic tableware destined for landfills or the sea. Disposable tableware such as tableware, containers and straws are among Hong Kong's top 10 sources of Marine waste, according to WWF Hong Kong's Coastal Observatory.

信息3：垃圾填埋场丢弃的塑料餐具在四年内增加了30%，从每天131公吨增加到169公吨。
Discarded plastic dining ware at landfills has increased about 30 percent in four years, from 131 to 169 metric tonnes per day.

Municipal solid waste (MSW) per person disposed at the landfills has increased 20 percent, from 1.27 kilograms in 2011 to 1.53 kilograms in 2018. It renders the government’s goal of reducing the MSW per person to 0.8 kilograms in 2020 unrealistic.
